    • Chapter 2: The Application Process at Fredonia

    Apply

    Once you've decided Fredonia could be the place for you, you may begin to apply the first week in August.

  • Complete the Application (SUNY Application OR Common Application)
  • Personal Essay (SUNY Application Part II)
  • Official High School Transcripts
  • SAT and/or ACT Scores (from the Testing Center or High School)
      • CEEB Code and SAT Code: 2539
      ACT Code: 2934
  • At least (1) Letter of Recommendation

    • Early Decision

    If you are 100% sure Fredonia is the place for you, you may guarentee your spot by applying Early Decision.

  • Select Early Decision on the application
  • Must complete application by Novemeber 1st
  • Must pay deposit by January 15th
  • Once a student is admitted through Early Decision, they are asked to withdraw all other college applications, as Early Decision is a binding agreement.
