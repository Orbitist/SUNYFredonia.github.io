Are you interested in bringing a group to campus?

Complete our Group Visit Registration Form to begin the process.

Generally, Monday mornings are reserved for large groups, but we are happy to make accommodations for other days and times. Group requests should be made two weeks in advance.

Accommodations for changes made less than 48 hours before your visit may not be feasible.

Please notify group visit coordinator Jessica Gatto of any changes as soon as possible.

Visits typically consist of:
    An hour-long information session with an admissions counselor
    A 90-minute walking tour of campus

*Accommodations can be made to eat on campus if needed; we can provide you with lunch options and prices.
