Join us at an Open House Event!

Open House Events provide an opportunity for prospective students and families to meet with academic departments, mingle with representatives from various student service departments, listen to a financial aid session, have lunch and then depart on a 90-minute walking tour of campus. Program's run 11 a.m. through 3 p.m.
