Fredonia Admissions

Come to Fredonia! Because... we're awesome!

Freshman

You are a freshman applicant if you are currently enrolled in high school, or if you have graduated from high school, but have not taken any college courses since.

More info

Transfer

You are a transfer student if you have taken any college courses after graduating from high school. Students who take courses for college-credit during high school but have not yet graduated are considered freshmen.

More info

International

International Students from all over the globe choose Fredonia for its high quality education, affordable price, private college-style campus, and friendly people. Students recognize the value of small classes and big opportunities to conduct research with fellow students and professors. Furthermore, they know the personal and professional connections they make here will lead them to a successful future.
